===============================================================================
DESIGN AREA TRACE-00 - TRACING, DIAGNOSTICS AND OBSERVABILITY INDEX
===============================================================================

Status:
-------
EVOLVING

Description:
------------
Defines the canonical entrypoint for the tracing design area family.

Purpose:
--------
This document routes the connector-safe tracing design area documents that
replace the former monolithic DA-03 tracing document.

This document defines:
- the tracing design area family
- the canonical child document list
- the former DA-03 section mapping
- the family-level interpretation boundary

This document does NOT define:
- trace event structure
- trace DAG semantics
- span mechanics
- reason or attribute field semantics
- trace retention or loss evidence
- user-facing diagnostic behavior
- domain-specific trace obligations

===============================================================================
01. FAMILY ROLE
===============================================================================

01.1. The tracing design area is represented by the directory:

      design/area/tracing/

01.2. TRACE-00 is the canonical family index for tracing, diagnostics, and
      observability.

01.3. TRACE-00 routes readers to connector-safe authority slices.

01.4. TRACE-00 does not replace the child documents that define tracing
      semantics.

01.5. TRACE-00 preserves the design-area boundary formerly held by DA-03.


===============================================================================
02. CANONICAL CHILD DOCUMENTS
===============================================================================

02.1. TRACE-01 defines trace substrate authority and substrate ownership.

02.2. TRACE-02 defines trace event model, trace DAG, and parent links.

02.3. TRACE-03 defines trace spans, roots, and span stratification.

02.4. TRACE-04 defines trace reason and trace attribute field semantics.

02.5. TRACE-05 defines trace retention, loss evidence, and trace access.

02.6. TRACE-06 defines user-facing diagnostics, error correlation, and
      development-mode trace enhancement.


===============================================================================
03. LEGACY DA-03 SECTION MAP
===============================================================================

03.1. Former DA-03 sections 01 through 03 map to TRACE-01.

03.2. Former DA-03 sections 04 through 06 map to TRACE-02.

03.3. Former DA-03 sections 07 through 08 map to TRACE-03.

03.4. Former DA-03 sections 09 through 10 map to TRACE-04.

03.5. Former DA-03 sections 11 through 12 map to TRACE-05.

03.6. Former DA-03 sections 13 through 18 map to TRACE-06.

03.7. Former DA-03 section 19 maps to TRACE-00 and to the locked summaries
      carried by child documents.


===============================================================================
04. FAMILY BOUNDARY
===============================================================================

04.1. The TRACE family owns tracing substrate semantics.

04.2. The TRACE family does not own object-specific lifecycle truth.

04.3. The TRACE family does not define which events each domain must emit.

04.4. Subject-owning design areas define subject-specific trace obligations.

04.5. Subject-owned trace obligations remain constrained by TRACE-family
      mechanics.

04.6. Moving DA-03 content into the TRACE family changes document structure
      and canonical identifiers.

04.7. Moving DA-03 content into the TRACE family does not intentionally
      weaken preserved tracing semantics.


===============================================================================
05. CONNECTOR-SAFE DOCUMENT FAMILY RULE
===============================================================================

05.1. A design area may be represented by a directory containing multiple
      registered authority slices.

05.2. Each authority slice remains small enough to be retrieved and updated
      through the repository connector as one complete file.

05.3. The family index preserves conceptual unity without requiring a single
      oversized file.


===============================================================================
06. LOCKED SUMMARY
===============================================================================

06.1. LOCKED:
      - tracing is now modeled as a design area family
      - TRACE-00 is the family entrypoint
      - TRACE-01 through TRACE-06 are connector-safe authority slices
      - DA-03 is the former monolithic tracing document
      - the TRACE family owns tracing substrate semantics
      - domain-specific trace obligations remain in subject-owning documents


===============================================================================
